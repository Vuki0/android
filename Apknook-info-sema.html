Game description:Dead ringer: Fear yourself

Dead ringer: Fear yourself - penetrate a secret base controlled by a sinister artificial intelligence. Fight against horrible monsters and destroy the machine intelligence. In this Android game you are going to deal with the results of scary experiments of a brilliant scientist. Artificial intelligence, created by the scientist, threatens the human race. Move across the rooms of the base. Deactivate alarm system, open locks and computer terminals. Gather tidbits of important information. Apply a shock rifle to fight against different mutants. Upgrade hero's munitions.

  • Hight quality music and sound
  • Hard missions
